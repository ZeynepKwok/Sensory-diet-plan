What Should I Do?

Steps to Support Your Child

Seek Professional Help: The first and most important step is to consult with medical professionals, especially an occupational therapist (OT). An OT can assess your child’s sensory needs and develop a personalized intervention plan that’s tailored specifically to them.

Using This Tool: While you’re seeking or waiting for professional guidance, you can use our Sensory Diet Plan Generator to create a supportive plan for your child. This tool is designed to help manage your child’s sensory needs in the meantime and can complement the advice you receive from an OT.
